Why HBO?

Frogger

This is the trailer for "Of Frogs 'n Gods", a game my group made last year, based on frogger. There were seven of us working on it, putting 200 hours each into it over the course of 8 weeks.
The idea is to get as far as possible without touching enemies or the water. The real challenge was adding voice control. We found it didn't work to control the frog due to a significant delay, so instead we adjusted the difficulty based on what the user was saying.

WorkingHours

WorkingHours is a quick little console app I wrote that I use to keep track of how many hours I've worked. It's limited in use, but some values like salary can be edited directly in the app.
It works by reading and writing timestamps to a file on my computer. I originally wrote it as practise with read/writing to files and as such it did it's job.
